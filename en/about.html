About MechTeam

MechTeam started development in early 2026 with a singular goal: to fuse the satisfaction of engineering with the chaotic gameplay of puzzle platformers. This game was inspired by a weird concoction of games such as: Bad Piggies, Monster Arena, Hill Climb Racing and Into the Breach. I want the game to be a digital garage for the tinkers and the giant-robot enthusiasts who believe that - some assembly required - is the best part of the box.

Where Engineering Meets Action

In the world of MechTeam, your creativity is your greatest weapon. Unlike traditional platformers where your abilities are set in stone, the modular mech-building system allows you to strip, swap, and calibrate every component of your machine to conquer unique environmental challenges.
